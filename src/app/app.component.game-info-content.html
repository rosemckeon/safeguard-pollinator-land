Gaming the acceptability of responses -
Policies for a pollinator friendly landscape

Your landscape is made of three habitat types, semi-natural, agricultural and urban. Each square on the landscape represents an area of land, for instance a large farm, or a city quarter.

The game demonstrates responses identified as effective to extremely effective by expert elicitation, and allows users to view outcomes in terms of how responses affect the habitat states (blue bars) and the resulting landscape impacts (yellow bars).

To find out more about any response, state, or impact, give them a click to find more detailed information.
